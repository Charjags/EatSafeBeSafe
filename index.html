EatSafeBeSafe

Have you ever wanted to walk inside a restaurant kitchen to see how clean it actually is? According to the World Health Organization, each year, over 600 million people get sick from foodborne illnesses while 33 million people die. We want to save lives and limit sicknesses by letting you know the ins and outs of what you eat. We present to you the EatSafeBeSafe. A map solely impressioned towards finding you the most accurate failed health inspections and providing you with the latest inspection score, date, violation type, and risk severity.
